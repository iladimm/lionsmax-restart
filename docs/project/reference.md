## 1. Project Overview

**LionsMax** is an AI-powered supplement recommendation platform tailored for adults aged 40–70. Our mission is to empower this demographic with trustworthy, evidence-based health supplement recommendations to support vitality, joint health, and longevity.

### Guiding Principles

- **MVP Inspiration:** The site design, product curation, and user experience will be heavily inspired by iHerb.com. Focus on a clean interface, clear product categories, and a simple search function.
- **Focus:** The initial release prioritizes core MVP features to ensure rapid launch.
- **Target Audience:** Adults 40–70 seeking preventive health, managing age-related changes, and pursuing longevity.

### Core Business Model

**Primary Revenue:** High-ticket affiliate programs (50%+ commission)  
**Secondary Revenue:** Amazon Associates Affiliate Marketing (1% commission on Health & Personal Care, optimized for high volume)  
**Year 1 Revenue Goal:** $3,000/month

## 3. UX & Style Instructions

### Overall Page Flow
**Complete section order:**
1. Top utility bar
2. Main header (logo + search + sign in/cart)
3. Primary navigation bar
4. Hero promo slider
5. Promo buttons (4 cards)
6. Special Offers carousel
7. Filter by Category section
8. Trending Today carousel
9. Brands of the Week section
10. Best Sellers carousel (with category filters)
11. New Arrivals carousel
12. Community highlight strip (optional)
13. Wellness Center (educational grid)
14. Rewards & value proposition banner
15. Footer (multi-column + newsletter)

### Design Principles
- Whitespace: Maintain generous whitespace; LionsMax should feel less crowded than iHerb
- Type Scale: Clean, readable typography with strong headings and legible body text
- Color Accent: Use accent colors sparingly to highlight deals, CTAs, and discounts
- Carousel Controls: Smooth horizontal scroll on desktop; touch-friendly on mobile. Show 4-6 products at a time (responsive)
- Buttons & CTAs: Consistent button styling for "Add to Cart", "Shop Now", "Learn More", "View All"
- Mobile Responsiveness: Optimize for mobile-first design. Stack sections vertically on small screens
- Accessibility: Ensure semantic HTML, ARIA labels, keyboard navigation, and proper contrast ratios
