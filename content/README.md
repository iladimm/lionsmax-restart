This folder contains all marketing and product content for LionsMax Restart.

## Structure

### Product Descriptions
`/products/` - Product descriptions, benefits, and specifications

### Lead Magnets
`/lead-magnets/` - Downloadable guides, e-books, and resources

### Email Campaigns
`/emails/` - Email templates and sequences

### Landing Pages
`/landing-pages/` - Landing page copy and CTAs

## Product Descriptions

Product descriptions should include:
- **Product Name**: Clear, SEO-friendly name
- **Short Description**: 2-3 sentences for listings
- **Long Description**: Detailed benefits and usage
- **Key Benefits**: Bullet points highlighting advantages
- **Ingredients**: Active ingredients and dosages
- **Suggested Use**: How to take the supplement
- **Target Audience**: Best for adults 40+, 50+, or 60+
- **SEO Keywords**: Primary and secondary keywords

### Example Products to Create:

## Content Guidelines

- **Tone**: Authoritative but approachable, evidence-based
- **Reading Level**: 8th-9th grade (accessible to all)
- **Focus**: Benefits over features
- **Evidence**: Cite studies where possible
- **SEO**: Include target keywords naturally
- **Mobile-First**: Keep paragraphs short and scannable
